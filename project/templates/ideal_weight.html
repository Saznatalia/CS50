How much a person should weigh is not an exact science. It is highly dependent on each individual. Thus far, there is no measure, be it Ideal Body Weight (IBW), body mass index (BMI), or any other that can definitively state how much a person should weigh to be healthy. They are only references, and it's more important to adhere to making healthy life choices such as regular exercise, eating a variety of unprocessed foods, getting enough sleep, etc. than it is to chase a specific weight based on a generalized formula. IBW formulas were developed mainly to facilitate drug dosage calculations.
