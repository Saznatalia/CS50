RULES OF BLOCK DIET

A block diet has a similar background as The Zone DietÂ® developed by Dr. Barry Sears. It encourages you to balance your nutrition in a caloric ratio of 30%-40%-30% (protein - carbohydrates - fats). The food block will always consist of protein, carbohydrate and fat of your choice. More information in Food Blocks.
  • The number of food blocks you should eat per day - 9! No more or less!
  • Start your day with a glass of water with 1 tbsp of apple cider vinegar or lemon juice.
  • Drink at least 2 liters of pure water daily.
  • The time between meals should not exceed 5 hours. The ideal time gap should be between 3 to 4 hours.
  • Exercise either 15-20 minutes every day or an hour 2-3 times a week.
  • Sleep at least 8 hours and include multivitamins.
